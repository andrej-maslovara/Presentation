  1. "Heroes of the Storm"


    Altough it's MOBA parents are more popular, to me it's an amazing game.
    Blizzard is really good at keeping it balanced and interesting,
    and that's why I prefer it over the others. Haven't played for a long time
    ever since I got kids basically, but this is the game I would go back to when
    I get some free time, also almost got to Grand Master rank when I stopped.


  2. "Hearthstone"


    Best card game out there, at least in digital form.
    Got to Legend rank a few times, but now I don't think
    I can play it anymore competitively since all the updates and new stuff.


  3. "Overwatch"


    Truly a revolutionary game as a FPS/MOBA hybrid, also would
    play again when I get the time.


  4. "Overcooked"


    Funny and exciting game to play with your friends, highly recommend :)


  5. "Hollow Knight"


    I played a lot of games Single and Multi player, and in my experience
    this game is truly amazing, so detailed, mysterious and yet so challenging
    but not so much that you want to quit it, just enough to push yourself
    to explore everyhing, unlock everything and defeat all enemies.
    When you dedicate yourself a little into it, you will find yourself
    lost in time playing it. I have never immersed myself into a Single player
    game as much as this one, that's why it deserves to be in the top tier list.
